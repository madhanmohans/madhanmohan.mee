## ==The WHY?:==
- What is the importance of building a second brain?
	- I have a belief in compound effect and wisdom.
	- I am tired of going about the day knowing that I will definitely forget what I am learning
	- My brain came to a point where it stopped producing novel ideas 
	- There are 2 main reasons I think
		- Over consumption
		- Reliance on stagnation (Giving up novelty)
	- My brain a couple of years back was an idea generating machine, I came up with whole systems of my own around productivity and Plans to execute.
	- The creative part of my brain was having fun learning things, and it was obsessed in the process of learning itself.
	- I want that part back and I want a reliable way to achieve that. I somehow think that this will free up mental space to allow more creative ways to solve problems of my life
