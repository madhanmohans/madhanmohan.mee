**Delaying,** besides helping in digesting an idea, ultimately allows a flow of events to happen to ease out the process itself. Let me explain.
  
In this world of instant connectivity and thrill, we are forced to make quick decisions every day to cope up, due to which we often find ourselves lost in the immediateness of things happening.
  
Having the right amount of headspace to navigate these situations and tough decision making has become crucial these days.
  
**Delaying and slowing down** becomes helpful here since it allows things to fall in place, to let the nature of things to help us before we tend to rush and clean slate the process to none, for at the end, the process is what matters.
