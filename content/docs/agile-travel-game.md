## Steps
### First Ceremony | Preparation Time
- Iteration Planning Meeting (IPM)
    - Go through the user stories
    - Understand
    - Prepare
    - Ask valid questions to POs
### Estimation
- Complexity estimation
- Not the time or effort of each individual
- When the scope changes from the client, we can work again on the estimation
### Signup
- One card per pair
- Self signup
- Pragmatic pairing
- If simple card, solo
- If complex card, trio
### Development
- Happens within the pair
- Take into account all the expectations and work
### Showcase
- Set up expectations
- Show completed tasks
- Get signoffs on those completed tasks
- Get expectations for the next iteration expected stories

## Retrospective
- Velocity and Raw velocity
- Story Points or Size estimation (Fibonacci numbers or power of numbers)
    - Solely based on the complexity of the story
### Agile Ceremonies
- Sprint Planning
- Daily stand-ups
- Iteration review
- Retrospectives

> At the start, BA + POs will fill up the backlogs  
  
> [!important]  
> POs has a final say (devs can discuss and clarify with them)  
  
> [!important]  
> Switch pair every 2 days
