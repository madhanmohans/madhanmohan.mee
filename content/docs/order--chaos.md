  
Yesterday, I wrote about the significance of contrast in relationships for holistic development.
Today, I want to share the contrast one has to cultivate within, the contrast being order and chaos. In this context, order is the known phenomena and chaos is the unknown phenomena.
By living with the perfect mix of both, one will live a fulfilling life. For example, if I listen to someone who delivers either everything that I already know or something that I can't even understand, in which case is a total mess for me, the speech will be of no use.
Likewise, if we carry out our lives in a way that we are already accustomed to, or in a way of total creative chaos without any routines being established, our life will be either stagnant or messy.
Establishing routines firmly and also providing room for the unknown in life, will possibly be the most appealing and interesting way to live.
