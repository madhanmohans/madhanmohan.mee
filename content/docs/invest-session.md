## User stories
- Concentrate on the user need
- Describe business value
- From what System should do → Functionality that will be valuable to a user

> [!important]  
> Invest is a mnemonic to write a backlog item (user story)  
### Independent
- Can be developed on its own and in any order
- At least, in each iteration
### Negotiable
- Changed and rewritten up until part of an iteration
### Valuable
- Slicing
- Add value to the business
- Why are we building it? Who are we building for?
### Estimable
- Prioritise the cards  
    Barriers for estimation  
    
- Story being too big
- Not enough information
- Lack of domain knowledge or technical knowledge
- “Spike” stories, to tackle technical knowledge gap
### Small & Scalable
- Stories should take not weeks
