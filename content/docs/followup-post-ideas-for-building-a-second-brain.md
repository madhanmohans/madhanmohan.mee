## ==*Followup post ideas for building a second brain ==

Outsource your brain and focus on meaningful work and less distracted life.

---

The initial goal was not to do some Generative Artificial Intelligence on top of this. Yet, using Gen AI, I believe that I can make my second brain essentially DREAM of new connections or finding hidden patterns.

---

Deeper flow states should not be interrupted by you constantly distracting your brain to remember a single [[Thought - Task - Term]]. Novelty hits at random times of a day.

In that way, instead of constantly making my brain repeat a task in mind to remember, it will have free space to acomodate novel thoughts and creative solutions for my everyday problems.

One intentional pause allows the breathing space for our brain to think of that solution.
